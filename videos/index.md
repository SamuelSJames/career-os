## Video workflow (keep it simple)
1. Build the lab (study + understand).
2. Film while it's fresh — screen + voice, lean on the diagram.
3. Keep it short (Shorts-friendly), one clear idea per video.
4. Publish, log it here, increment the counter in PROGRESS.md.

**Bank rule:** in Oct–mid Nov, record ~2/week and schedule-publish to cover the
Thanksgiving→Christmas break.
